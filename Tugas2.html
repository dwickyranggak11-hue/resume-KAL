\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c & d & e \\ f & g & h & i & j \\ k & l & m & n & o \\ p & q & r & s & t \\ u & v & w & x & y \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Operasi Determinan Matriks#

Determinan didefinisikan sebagai berikut:

\[ \det(A) = \sum_{j=1}^{5} (-1)^{1+j} \, a_{1j} \, \det(A_{1j}) \]
\[ \det(A) = (-1)^{1+1} \, a \, \det(A_{11}) + (-1)^{1+2} \, b \, \det(A_{12}) + (-1)^{1+3} \, c \, \det(A_{13}) + (-1)^{1+4} \, d \, \det(A_{14}) + (-1)^{1+5} \, e \, \det(A_{15}) \]
\[ \det(A) = a \, \det(A_{11}) - b \, \det(A_{12}) + c \, \det(A_{13}) - d \, \det(A_{14}) + e \, \det(A_{15}) \]
\[\begin{split} \det(A) = a \, \det \begin{bmatrix} g & h & i & j \\ l & m & n & o \\ q & r & s & t \\ v & w & x & y \end{bmatrix} - b \, \det \begin{bmatrix} f & h & i & j \\ k & m & n & o \\ p & r & s & t \\ u & w & x & y \end{bmatrix} + c \, \det \begin{bmatrix} f & g & i & j \\ k & l & n & o \\ p & q & s & t \\ u & v & x & y \end{bmatrix} - d \, \det \begin{bmatrix} f & g & h & j \\ k & l & m & o \\ p & q & r & t \\ u & v & w & y \end{bmatrix} + e \, \det \begin{bmatrix} f & g & h & i \\ k & l & m & n \\ p & q & r & s \\ u & v & w & x \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Bagian \( a \, \det(A_{11})\)#

\[\begin{split} a \, \det(A_{11}) = a \begin{vmatrix} g & h & i & j \\ l & m & n & o \\ q & r & s & t \\ v & w & x & y \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]

\[\begin{split} = a \left[ g \begin{vmatrix} m & n & o \\ r & s & t \\ w & x & y \end{vmatrix} - h \begin{vmatrix} l & n & o \\ q & s & t \\ v & x & y \end{vmatrix} + i \begin{vmatrix} l & m & o \\ q & r & t \\ v & w & y \end{vmatrix} - j \begin{vmatrix} l & m & n \\ q & r & s \\ v & w & x \end{vmatrix} \right] \end{split}\]

Ekspansi kofaktor menjadi matriks 2x2

\[\begin{split} = a \left[ g \left( m \begin{vmatrix} s & t \\ x & y \end{vmatrix} - n \begin{vmatrix} r & t \\ w & y \end{vmatrix} + o \begin{vmatrix} r & s \\ w & x \end{vmatrix} \right) - h \left( l \begin{vmatrix} s & t \\ x & y \end{vmatrix} - n \begin{vmatrix} q & t \\ v & y \end{vmatrix} + o \begin{vmatrix} q & s \\ v & x \end{vmatrix} \right) + i \left( l \begin{vmatrix} r & t \\ w & y \end{vmatrix} - m \begin{vmatrix} q & t \\ v & y \end{vmatrix} + o \begin{vmatrix} q & r \\ v & w \end{vmatrix} \right) - j \left( l \begin{vmatrix} r & s \\ w & x \end{vmatrix} - m \begin{vmatrix} q & s \\ v & x \end{vmatrix} + n \begin{vmatrix} q & r \\ v & w \end{vmatrix} \right) \right] \end{split}\]

\[ = a \Big( g \big( m(sy - tx) - n(ry - tw) + o(rx - sw) \big) - h \big( l(sy - tx) - n(qy - tv) + o(qx - sv) \big) + i \big( l(ry - tw) - m(qy - tv) + o(qw - rv) \big) - j \big( l(rx - sw) - m(qx - sv) + n(qw - rv) \big) \Big) \]
