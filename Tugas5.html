Eigen Value dan Eigen Vector#

Link Colab Program Pyhton: https://colab.research.google.com/drive/1q8F1_UzZpoYex3Zvdg-y2MZ7boE4UXiB?usp=sharing

Diketahui matriks:

\[\begin{split} A= \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1\\ 1 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Metode QR Iteration digunakan untuk mencari eigenvalue suatu matriks.

Prinsip dasar metode QR:

  1. Faktorkan matriks menjadi:

\[ A_k = Q_kR_k \]

dengan:

  • \(Q_k\) = matriks ortogonal

  • \(R_k\) = matriks segitiga atas

  1. Bentuk matriks baru:

\[ A_{k+1}=R_kQ_k \]

  1. Ulangi proses tersebut hingga elemen di luar diagonal mendekati nol.

ITERASI 0#

Matriks awal:

\[\begin{split} A_0= \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1\\ 1 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 1 — Menentukan Vektor Kolom#

Kolom pertama:

\[\begin{split} a_1= \begin{bmatrix} 2\\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Kolom kedua:

\[\begin{split} a_2= \begin{bmatrix} 1\\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 2 — Mencari \(q_1\)#

Rumus:

\[ q_1=\frac{a_1}{||a_1||} \]

Menghitung panjang \(a_1\):

\[ ||a_1||= \sqrt{2^2+1^2} \]
\[ = \sqrt{4+1} \]
\[ = \sqrt5 \]

Maka:

\[\begin{split} q_1= \begin{bmatrix} \frac2{\sqrt5}\\ \frac1{\sqrt5} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 3 — Mencari \(v_2\)#

Rumus Gram-Schmidt:

\[ v_2=a_2-(q_1\cdot a_2)q_1 \]

Menghitung \(q_1 \cdot a_2\)#

\[ \left(\frac2{\sqrt5}\times1\right) + \left(\frac1{\sqrt5}\times2\right) \]
\[ = \frac2{\sqrt5} + \frac2{\sqrt5} \]
\[ = \frac4{\sqrt5} \]

Menghitung \((q_1 \cdot a_2)q_1\)#

\[\begin{split} \frac4{\sqrt5} \begin{bmatrix} \frac2{\sqrt5}\\ \frac1{\sqrt5} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac85\\ \frac45 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Menghitung \(v_2\)#

\[\begin{split} v_2= \begin{bmatrix} 1\\ 2 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} \frac85\\ \frac45 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} = \begin{bmatrix} -\frac35\\ \frac65 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 4 — Mencari \(q_2\)#

Rumus:

\[ q_2=\frac{v_2}{||v_2||} \]

Menghitung panjang \(v_2\)#

\[ ||v_2||= \sqrt{ \left(-\frac35\right)^2+ \left(\frac65\right)^2 } \]
\[ = \sqrt{ \frac9{25}+\frac{36}{25} } \]
\[ = \sqrt{\frac{45}{25}} \]
\[ = \frac3{\sqrt5} \]

Menghitung \(q_2\)#

\[\begin{split} q_2= \begin{bmatrix} -\frac35\\ \frac65 \end{bmatrix} \div \frac3{\sqrt5} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} = \begin{bmatrix} -\frac1{\sqrt5}\\ \frac2{\sqrt5} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 5 — Membentuk Matriks \(Q\)#

\[ Q_0=(q_1,q_2) \]
\[\begin{split} Q_0= \begin{bmatrix} \frac2{\sqrt5} & -\frac1{\sqrt5}\\ \frac1{\sqrt5} & \frac2{\sqrt5} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 6 — Membentuk Matriks \(R\)#

Rumus:

\[\begin{split} R= \begin{bmatrix} a_1\cdot q_1 & a_2\cdot q_1\\ 0 & a_2\cdot q_2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Menghitung \(a_1 \cdot q_1\)#

\[ (2\times\frac2{\sqrt5}) + (1\times\frac1{\sqrt5}) \]
\[ = \frac4{\sqrt5}+\frac1{\sqrt5} \]
\[ = \frac5{\sqrt5} \]
\[ = \sqrt5 \]

Menghitung \(a_2 \cdot q_1\)#

\[ (1\times\frac2{\sqrt5}) + (2\times\frac1{\sqrt5}) \]
\[ = \frac2{\sqrt5}+\frac2{\sqrt5} \]
\[ = \frac4{\sqrt5} \]

Menghitung \(a_2 \cdot q_2\)#

\[ (1\times-\frac1{\sqrt5}) + (2\times\frac2{\sqrt5}) \]
\[ = -\frac1{\sqrt5}+\frac4{\sqrt5} \]
\[ = \frac3{\sqrt5} \]

Matriks \(R\)#

\[\begin{split} R_0= \begin{bmatrix} \sqrt5 & \frac4{\sqrt5}\\ 0 & \frac3{\sqrt5} \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Langkah 7 — Menghitung \(A_1\)#

Rumus:

\[ A_1=R_0Q_0 \]

Elemen (1,1)#

\[ \left(\sqrt5\times\frac2{\sqrt5}\right) + \left(\frac4{\sqrt5}\times\frac1{\sqrt5}\right) \]
\[ = 2+\frac45 \]
\[ = \frac{14}5 \]
\[ = 2.8 \]

Elemen (1,2)#

\[ \left(\sqrt5\times-\frac1{\sqrt5}\right) + \left(\frac4{\sqrt5}\times\frac2{\sqrt5}\right) \]
\[ = -1+\frac85 \]
\[ = \frac35 \]
\[ = 0.6 \]

Elemen (2,1)#

\[ \left(0\times\frac2{\sqrt5}\right) + \left(\frac3{\sqrt5}\times\frac1{\sqrt5}\right) \]
\[ = 0+\frac35 \]
\[ = 0.6 \]

Elemen (2,2)#

\[ \left(0\times-\frac1{\sqrt5}\right) + \left(\frac3{\sqrt5}\times\frac2{\sqrt5}\right) \]
\[ = 0+\frac65 \]
\[ = 1.2 \]

Hasil Iterasi Pertama#

\[\begin{split} A_1= \begin{bmatrix} 2.8 & 0.6\\ 0.6 & 1.2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

ITERASI 1#

Diketahui:

\[\begin{split} A_1= \begin{bmatrix} 2.8 & 0.6\\ 0.6 & 1.2 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Lakukan dekomposisi QR:

\[ A_1=Q_1R_1 \]

Kemudian hitung:

\[ A_2=R_1Q_1 \]

Hasil:

\[\begin{split} A_2\approx \begin{bmatrix} 2.9756 & 0.2195\\ 0.2195 & 1.0244 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Terlihat bahwa elemen luar diagonal mulai mengecil:

\[ 0.6 \rightarrow 0.2195 \]

ITERASI 2#

Gunakan hasil sebelumnya:

\[\begin{split} A_2\approx \begin{bmatrix} 2.9756 & 0.2195\\ 0.2195 & 1.0244 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Lakukan lagi:

\[ A_2=Q_2R_2 \]

Kemudian:

\[ A_3=R_2Q_2 \]

Hasil:

\[\begin{split} A_3\approx \begin{bmatrix} 2.9973 & 0.0739\\ 0.0739 & 1.0027 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Elemen luar diagonal semakin kecil:

\[ 0.2195 \rightarrow 0.0739 \]

ITERASI 3#

Diketahui:

\[\begin{split} A_3\approx \begin{bmatrix} 2.9973 & 0.0739\\ 0.0739 & 1.0027 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Lakukan:

\[ A_3=Q_3R_3 \]

Kemudian:

\[ A_4=R_3Q_3 \]

Hasil:

\[\begin{split} A_4\approx \begin{bmatrix} 2.9997 & 0.0247\\ 0.0247 & 1.0003 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Elemen luar diagonal:

\[ 0.0739 \rightarrow 0.0247 \]

Kesimpulan#

Semakin banyak iterasi QR dilakukan:

  • elemen luar diagonal semakin mendekati nol

  • elemen diagonal semakin mendekati eigenvalue

Hasil akhir:

\[\begin{split} A_{10}\approx \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

Maka eigenvalue matriks:

\[ \lambda_1=3 \]

dan

\[ \lambda_2=1 \]
